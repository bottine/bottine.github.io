My email

I received a PhD in mathematics at the université of Neuchâtel, Switzerland in October 2021 (supervised by Sasha Kolpakov). Before that, I did a bachelor in computer science and a master in mathematics, both at the EPFL, Lausanne, Switzerland.

I'm mainly interested in geometric group theory, coarse geometry, foundations of mathematics (computability, categorical logic, type theory) and formalisation of mathematics.

Software

  • CoxeterDiagrams a julia library for computing some properties of hyperbolic Coxeter diagrams (respectively the associated polytope). Based on Raphael Guglielmetti's Coxiter.
  • Vinny a julia library for constructing fundamental polyhedra of hyperbolic reflection groups.
  • nem some rust code for enumerating Spehner pavings on a given number of darts (used in the “Three-dimensional maps and subgroup growth” paper).
